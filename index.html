PILLPING

AI-Powered Medicine Reminder & Restock Management

A smart healthcare solution designed for Pakistani patients to improve medication adherence, prevent missed doses, and intelligently manage refills using AI, AR & predictive systems.

AI Chatbot AR Recognition SMS + Push Alerts Caregiver Support

Introduction

Medication non-adherence is a serious healthcare issue in Pakistan, especially for chronic patients suffering from diabetes, heart disease, asthma, and hypertension.

PILLPING addresses this challenge by combining intelligent reminders, refill forecasting, AR-based medicine recognition, multilingual AI assistance, and caregiver monitoring — all tailored for local needs.

Core Features

AI Chatbot Assistance

Provides medication guidance, missed-dose instructions, and answers in Urdu & English.

AR Medicine Recognition

Scan medicine packaging to identify dosage, warnings, and instructions instantly.

Smart Restock Forecasting

Predicts depletion, estimates cost, and alerts before medicines run out.

Caregiver Support

Family members can monitor dependent patients’ medication compliance.

Multi-Channel Reminders

Push notifications + SMS alerts for low-internet or rural areas.

Multi-Lingual Support

Designed for users with low literacy using simple language & visuals.

Objectives

  • ✔ Improve medication adherence for Pakistani patients
  • ✔ Predict medicine depletion before shortages occur
  • ✔ Provide AI-based medication guidance
  • ✔ Enable caregiver monitoring
  • ✔ Support AR-based medicine scanning
  • ✔ Ensure accessibility via SMS reminders
